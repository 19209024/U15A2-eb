WORK FOR US

Why work for us?

Working at Hot Beans Web brings many advantages. We have strong conections with leading organisations across the software industry, most notably Microsoft, Google, and Adobe. We strive to create not just websites, but experiences for our clients. No matter your level of expertise, we will provide you with industry-level training to turn you into the best developer you can be.

Specification

Junior Front-End Developer

Starting Salary: £25,000

As a junior front-end developer at Hot Beans Web, you will...

  • Assist in the development of responsive web pages and user interfaces.
  • Implement visual elements and ensure they are functional on all screen sizes.
  • Collaborate with website designers and senior developers to refine user experience and interface layouts & design.
  • Participate in code reviews and meetings.
  • Use front-end libraries and frameworks.
  • Test and debug code to ensure applications are functioning as intended.
  • Participate in learning and development opportunities to improve on and grow technical skills.

Senior Front-End Developer

Starting Salary: £55,000 (Variable)

As a senior front-end developer at Hot Beans Web, you will...

  • Lead the development of high-performance front-end experiences.
  • Collaborate with design teams and developers from across the industry to create intuitive and appealing user interfaces and layouts.
  • Mentor junior developers, define coding standards and practices for a team, and provide support where necessary.
  • Use front-end frameworks and libraries.
  • Ensure cross-platform compatability and browser compliance.
  • Participate and lead in code reviews.

We have no other jobs available at the moment. Check back soon, or contact us for specific details.

Qualifications

Junior Front-End Developer

  • Achieved a Level 3 qualification or higher in Web Development, Computer Science, or other relevant qualifications
  • Knowledge of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.
  • Knowledge of Javascript frameworks
  • Understanding of Search Engine Optimisation
  • Knowledge of Git

Senior Front-End Developer

    All of the above, as well as...

  • Achieved a Level 3 qualification or higher in Web Development, Computer Science, or other relevant qualifications
  • A minimum of 5 years of industry experience
  • Expert in API integration
  • Proficient at Search Engine Optimisation
  • Proficient in Git
  • Project management and team leadership skills

Extra Information

If you lack a relevant qualification but still want to join us, we may also accept developers with a portfolio of projects showcasing skills that are relevant to our work. Our "useful resources" page provides you with all courses that are relevant to our work. Please note however that we prioritise qualified applicants so this may not guarantee you a position. Do not hesistate to contact us if you have any questions or concerns regarding our requirements, as we are open to offering positions depending on your situation.
